Services we're offering

Measurements

we specialize in capturing the precise dimensions and proportions of your body to create custom-fit clothing that flatters your shape and reflects your personal style. Whether it’s for a one-of-a-kind design or a flawless fit, our expert measurement process ensures every piece is made to perfection.

Repair

Whether it's fixing a tear, adjusting the fit, or reworking a design, our expert repair process ensures your clothes are brought back to life with precision and care. We focus on maintaining the integrity of the original piece while enhancing its style and functionality.

Bouquet

Our custom wedding bouquets are designed with the finest blooms, handpicked to create a stunning floral centerpiece that enhances your special day and reflects your unique vision.

Traditional Fan

Crafted with intricate designs and high-quality materials, it symbolizes grace and cultural heritage. Perfect for both function and style, a wedding fan can complement the bride's attire, adding a personal touch while keeping cool in the spotlight.

Sewing

We turn creative ideas into wearable garments. involving pattern making, fabric cutting, and stitching with precision. By sewing, designing perfect fits, customizing designs, and bring your unique visions to life with high-quality craftsmanship.

Restoration

we are involve in the process of repairing, renewing, or preserving objects, structures, or artworks to their original condition.

Сuttings

We turn fabric into perfect patterns. From simple styles to detailed designs, our cloth cutting is neat, accurate, and ready for the perfect fit.

Beading

We create unique, handcrafted items using beautiful beads, from jewelry to decor. Explore our collection and find the perfect piece made with care and creativity.
